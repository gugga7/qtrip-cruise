How does the user refine the AI plan?

The AI suggests a full shore plan — then the user makes it theirs

A

Card Swap: Tap to replace

Each activity in the plan is a card. Tap "Swap" to see 2-3 alternatives for that slot (same time, same vibe, similar price). Pick one or keep the original.

☀️ MORNING 8:00–11:00
Gothic Quarter Walk
3h · €35/person
🌤️ AFTERNOON 11:30–15:00
La Boqueria Food Tour
3.5h · €55/person
⚓ All aboard by 16:30 · Total: €90/person

Simple, fast, visual. Works great on mobile. Limited to pre-curated alternatives.

B

Chat Refinement: Talk to the concierge

Below the generated plan, a chat input lets the user ask for changes in natural language:

Your Barcelona Plan (8am–5pm)
☀️ Gothic Quarter Walk · 🌤️ La Boqueria Food Tour
Try: "Add something for the kids" · "Make it more relaxed" · "I want to try paella"

Maximum flexibility. Feels like talking to a travel agent. Requires real-time AI calls.

C

Both: Cards + Chat

Show the plan as swappable cards (option A) with a chat bar at the bottom (option B). Quick swaps for simple changes, chat for complex requests.

The cards handle 80% of refinements (swap, remove, reorder). Chat handles the creative stuff ("I heard there's a great tapas place near the port" or "We have a wheelchair user").

Best UX but most engineering. Card swaps are instant (local), chat triggers AI re-generation.
