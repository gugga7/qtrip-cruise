How much input before AI generates?

The concierge needs enough context to be useful — but less input = more magic

A

Minimal: Just the cruise itinerary

User enters: ports + dates + dock times + group size. That's it.

AI infers preferences from popular activities and generates a balanced plan. User refines after.

Input: "Barcelona (8am-5pm), Dubrovnik (7am-2pm), 4 people"
AI output: Full plan with activities, timing, costs

B

Light Preferences: Itinerary + vibe/budget

User enters: ports + dock times + group size + budget per port + activity vibe (e.g., "cultural", "adventure", "foodie", "relaxed").

AI uses the vibe + budget to pick the right mix. More relevant first suggestions, less refinement needed.

Input: "Barcelona (8am-5pm), 4 people, €200 budget, 'foodie + culture'"
AI output: Morning: Gothic Quarter walking tour, Afternoon: La Boqueria food tour

C

Smart Onboarding: Quick quiz then AI

3-4 quick visual questions before generating:

  • 🎯 What matters most? (culture / food / adventure / relaxation / shopping)
  • 🏃 Activity level? (easy stroll / moderate / active explorer)
  • 💰 Budget per port? (slider: budget / mid-range / premium)
  • 👥 Group type? (couple / family / friends / solo)

Feels like a personal concierge learning about you. AI generates a highly tailored plan.

Result: "Based on your love of food and moderate activity level, here's your Barcelona day..."
